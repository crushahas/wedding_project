Ceremony Location

Catedral Metropolitana de la Ciudad de México

Pza de la Constitución S/N
Centro Histórico de la Cdad. de México
Centro, Cuauhtémoc, 06000
Ciudad de México, CDMX, Mexico

This historic cathedral is in the heart of the Central Historic District. From Wikepedia: It is situated atop the former Aztec sacred precinct near the Templo Mayor on the northern side of the Plaza de la Constitución (Zócalo) in Downtown Mexico City. The cathedral was built in sections from 1573 to 1813 around the original church that was constructed soon after the Spanish conquest of Tenochtitlan, eventually replacing it entirely. Spanish architect Claudio de Arciniega planned the construction, drawing inspiration from Gothic cathedrals in Spain.

Gran Hotel Ciudad de México

16 de Septiembre 82
Centro Histórico de la Cdad. de México
Centro, Cuauhtémoc
06000 Ciudad de México
CDMX, Mexico

This hotel is located just 350 meters from the cathedral. Staying their overnight will enable yourself to see all of Centro's famous sites including the Plaza de la Constitucion and the National Palace. From the hotel's website: The origin of this historic building dates back to 1526. It was a residence to the Royal Accountant, Rodrigo de Albornoz. In 1895, it was bought by the Frenchman Sebastian Robert to make the first shopping center in Mexico.
